# Acronym Glossary — Pristina Waste System

> Single-source reference for the acronyms used in this dossier folder. Many overlap across documents and a few have outright collisions (notably "KAS" and "PPP"). When in doubt, check this file.

## Government — Kosovo

| Acronym | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **MMPHI** | Ministria e Mjedisit, Planifikimit Hapësinor dhe Infrastrukturës — Ministry of Environment, Spatial Planning and Infrastructure (lead waste-policy authority). Albanian form. |
| **MESPI** | Ministry of Environment, Spatial Planning and Infrastructure — English form used by GIZ + EU partners. **Same institution as MMPHI**; cross-reference only. |
| **MLGA** | Ministry of Local Government Administration |
| **MRD** | Ministry of Regional Development |
| **AMMK** | Agjencia për Mbrojtjen e Mjedisit të Kosovës — Kosovo Environmental Protection Agency (also written **KEPA** in some English sources). Operates under MMPHI. |
| **MINT** | Ministria e Industrisë, Ndërmarrësisë dhe Tregtisë — Ministry of Industry, Entrepreneurship and Trade |
| **KAS** (1) | Agjencia e Statistikave të Kosovës — Kosovo Agency of Statistics. ⚠ Collides with KAS (2) below. |
| **NIPHK / IKSHPK** | National Institute of Public Health of Kosovo |
| **KEK** | Korporata Energjetike e Kosovës — Kosovo Energy Corporation. Owns the land Mirash sits on. |
| **Hybrid Regulator** | Proposed new institutional actor in the upcoming Law on Integrated Waste Management. Tariff setting, performance monitoring, dispute resolution. |

## Operators

| Acronym | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **KLMC / KMDK** | Kosovo Landfill Management Company — Kompania për Menaxhimin e Deponive të Kosovës. Operates the seven public landfills including Mirash. |
| **Pastrimi** (KRM) | Regional Waste Collection Company "Pastrimi" JSC — primary collector in Pristina region. |
| **Ambienti** | Regional waste collection company (Pristina region — surfaced in INDEP/KAS 2026, not in original dossier). |
| **Ekoregjioni** | Regional waste collection company (Prizren region; receives JICA grant trucks). |

## Technical / policy concepts

| Acronym | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **EPR** | Extended Producer Responsibility |
| **DRS / SRD** | Deposit Refund System (English / Albanian: Sistemi i Rimbursimit të Depozitave). Planned launch Jan 2025; current launch status unverified as of May 2026. |
| **GPP** | Green Public Procurement |
| **PRO** | Producer Responsibility Organization (under EPR systems) |
| **FNCC** | Full Net Cost Coverage. New principle in upcoming law: collection / transport / recycling / treatment fully covered by user/producer fees. |
| **PPP** (1) | Polluter Pays Principle. ⚠ Collides with PPP (2) below. |
| **PPP** (2) | Public-Private Partnership. ⚠ Collides with PPP (1) above — disambiguate from context (waste-policy text usually means Polluter Pays; finance/procurement text usually means Public-Private). |
| **PAYT** | Pay As You Throw. |
| **SaS** | Source-as-Separate (a.k.a. source separation). |
| **3Rs** | Reduce, Reuse, Recycle. |
